Designing for High Availability Without OverEngineering

TechStorm · Virtual, Ghana · May 2026

Event overview

Reliability by design, not by accidental complexity

This webinar unpacked practical reliability patterns for modern systems. The focus was on balancing uptime goals with implementation simplicity, helping teams avoid over-engineering while still meeting production resilience targets.

  • Mapping service criticality to realistic availability targets.
  • Designing fallback paths and dependency boundaries.
  • 5 + 1 reliability patterns for modern systems.
