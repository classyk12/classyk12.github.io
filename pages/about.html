About

Faith Sodipe is a software engineer based in the United Kingdom, with a sharp focus on systems that are both technically rigorous and deeply human-centred. He works at the intersection of product engineering, cybersecurity, and open-source tooling, building technology that is as thoughtful as it is functional.

He speaks at conferences across the UK and internationally, mentors emerging engineers, and writes about the culture and craft of software development, AI, and cybersecurity. He runs community initiatives designed to bring underrepresented voices into the rooms where decisions in tech are made.

Faith believes the best technology is built by people who care about the craft, about the user, and about each other.
