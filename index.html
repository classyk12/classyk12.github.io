About

I'm a software engineer with a sharp focus on systems that are both technically rigorous and deeply human-centred. I've spent years at the intersection of product engineering, open-source tooling, and community work in tech.

I speak at conferences, mentor emerging engineers, write about the culture and craft of software, and run initiatives that bring more people into the rooms where decisions are made.

I believe the best technology is built by people who care about the craft, about the user, and about each other.
